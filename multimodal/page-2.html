Thanks for your interest in Multimodal!

I'm excited to announce that the first full release of Multimodal will be on the App Store on October 1st, 2025. First release will provide coverage for all Los Angeles rapid transit lines. San Francisco is next.

This project comes from a deep belief: transit should be efficient and convenient. In so many American cities, majority of people live too far from train stations. This is commonly known as the "last mile" problem. However, most of these people are close enough to bike to the station!

Personally, I live a 10-minute bike ride from my local station. Walking would take 40 minutes, and the bus isn't much faster. Taking my bike on the train makes transit a viable option for me. Otherwise, it takes too long and driving is much faster.

The issue is: planning these trips is difficult with current navigation apps. Multimodal makes bike + transit planning feel effortless.

Get Multimodal to become a part of this movement.
