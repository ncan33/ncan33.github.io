Thanks for your interest in Multimodal!

I'm excited to announce that the first full release of Multimodal will be on the App Store on May 15th, 2025. First release will provide coverage for all Los Angeles rapid transit lines. San Francisco is next.

This project comes from a deep belief: transit should be efficient, accessible, and empowering. In so many American cities (such as Los Angeles), majority of people live too far from train stations to walk. This is commonly known as the "last mile" problem. However, most of these people are close enough to bike to the station! Personally, I live a 10-minute bike ride from my local station. Walking would take 40 minutes. That efficiency gain is everything.

Multimodal is my way of making that connection as easy and seamless as possible. I want to take the guesswork out of timing your trip, and make combining biking and transit feel effortless‚Äìbecause when it's easy, more people will do it. And when more people use transit, cities get better.
